# Legal Pages

Every public product needs a privacy policy and terms of service. These are starting templates, not legal advice. Have counsel review before launch, especially if you handle payments, health, or children's data.

## Required For Most Products

- Privacy Policy — what you collect, why, how long, who you share with, user rights.
- Terms of Service — acceptable use, accounts, payment terms, liability, termination.
- (If you set cookies beyond essentials) a cookie notice and consent.

## Templates

Use the bundled starting points and fill in the bracketed fields:

- `assets/templates/privacy-policy.md`
- `assets/templates/terms-of-service.md`

## Pages

- Render legal pages with the kit theme at a readable width (`max-w-2xl`, generous line length).
- Link them in the footer and on sign-up near the primary action.
- Show a "last updated" date and keep it current.

## Rules

- Make the copy match what the product actually does. Do not claim to not collect data you collect (`references/analytics.md`).
- Disclose payment processing (Stripe) and any third parties that receive user data.
- Provide a real contact method for privacy requests.
- Keep the language plain; legalese that no one reads protects no one.
